Brahma Purana

Brahma Purana ko kai baar Adi Purana bhi kaha jata hai. Isme mainly srishti ki rachna, prithvi ke bhaugolik varnan, aur devtaon ki vanshavali ka detail milta hai. Yeh Purana Brahma ji se sambandhit hai lekin isme Vishnu aur Shiva ki bhi mahima batayi gayi hai. Isme tirth yatra ka mahatva, khaaskar Jagannath Puri aur Konark jaise pavitra sthalon ka varnan milta hai. Is Purana me dharma, karma aur bhakti ke siddhant simple kahaniyon aur updeshon ke through samjhaye gaye hain. Rajdharma, grihastha jeevan aur punya-paap ke concept ko bhi explain kiya gaya hai. Iska ek important theme hai ki insaan ko satya aur dharm ke path par chalna chahiye. Brahma Purana puranik cosmology ka ek basic framework deta hai jisse baaki Puranon ko samajhne me bhi madad milti hai.

Padma Purana

Padma Purana ka naam kamal (padma) se juda hai jo Vishnu ji ki naabhi se nikle kamal ka symbol hai. Yeh Purana bahut bada hai aur alag-alag khandon me divided hai. Isme bhakti, tirth yatra, vrat aur sadachaar par khas zor diya gaya hai. Yeh batata hai ki kaise ek aam vyakti bhi bhakti aur achhe karmon se moksha ki taraf badh sakta hai. Isme Bhagwan Vishnu ki mahima ke saath-saath Devi aur Shiva ke roop ka bhi varnan milta hai. Padma Purana me prakriti aur dharti ko pavitra maana gaya hai, aur environment ki raksha ko dharm ka hissa bataya gaya hai. Kahaniyon ke through yeh sikhata hai ki vinamrata, daya aur shraddha insaan ke jeevan ko sundar banate hain.
