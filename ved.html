RIG-VED

Rigveda ko sabse ancient aur important Veda mana jata hai. Aisa maana jata hai ki prachin samay me divine knowledge rishiyon ko shruti ke roop me prapt hua, jise baad me Rigveda ke hymns me compile kiya gaya. Yeh Veda mainly devtaon ki stuti, nature ki powers aur cosmic order ko explain karta hai. Rigveda me total 10 mandalas (books) hote hain aur lagbhag 1,028 suktas ya hymns milte hain, jisme kareeb 10,000 se zyada mantras included hain. Isme Indra, Agni, Varuna aur Surya jaise devtaon ki prarthana aur unki mahima ka varnan milta hai. Rigveda sirf religious text nahi balki ancient Indian society, culture, science aur philosophy ka bhi ek important source mana jata hai. Iski language Vedic Sanskrit hai jo baad ki classical Sanskrit ka foundation bani. Overall, Rigveda ko Indian spiritual thought aur knowledge tradition ka starting point bhi kaha jata hai.

SAM-VED

Samaveda ko music aur melody ka Veda bhi kaha jata hai. Yeh mainly Rigveda ke hi mantron ka musical form hai, jise yajna ke time priests gaate the. Iski origin bhi Vedic age me hui aur ise Indian classical music ka earliest source mana jata hai. Samaveda me lagbhag 1,800 se zyada verses milte hain, lekin inme se bahut se Rigveda se liye gaye hain. Is Veda ka main purpose spiritual atmosphere create karna aur devotion ko sound aur rhythm ke through express karna tha. Samaveda ne na sirf religious rituals ko sundar banaya balki Indian music tradition ki foundation bhi strong ki. Isliye ise spiritual aur cultural dono perspective se kaafi significant mana jata hai.

YAJUR-VED

Yajurveda ko rituals aur yajna-vidhi ka Veda mana jata hai. Iska main focus practical worship system par hota hai, jisme priests ko bataya jata tha ki yajna aur religious ceremonies kaise perform karni hain. Yajurveda ki origin bhi ancient Vedic period me hui aur ise Rigvedic knowledge ka ritualistic extension kaha jata hai. Is Veda ke do main versions milte hain — Krishna (Black) Yajurveda aur Shukla (White) Yajurveda. Structure ki baat karein to isme lagbhag 40 adhyay (chapters) hote hain jisme mantras aur prose formulas dono milte hain. Yajurveda society me discipline, duty aur sacred action ki importance ko highlight karta hai. Yeh Veda ancient Indian religious life ko organize karne me bahut important role play karta tha aur spiritual practices ko systematic banata tha.

ATHARV-VED

Atharvaveda ko daily life aur practical knowledge ka Veda mana jata hai. Yeh dusre Vedas se thoda alag hai kyunki isme sirf yajna aur stuti hi nahi balki health, protection, peace aur social life se related mantras bhi milte hain. Iski origin later Vedic period me hui aur yeh common people ki needs se zyada connected mana jata hai. Atharvaveda me total 20 kand (books) hote hain jisme lagbhag 730 suktas aur kareeb 6,000 mantras milte hain. Isme healing prayers, philosophical ideas aur household rituals ka bhi varnan hai. Yeh Veda batata hai ki ancient Indian knowledge sirf spiritual hi nahi balki practical aur scientific thinking se bhi juda hua tha. Isliye Atharvaveda ko holistic life guidance ka source mana jata hai..
