Śikṣā

Shiksha Vedang Vedic mantron ki sahi pronunciation aur sound science se related hai. Isme swar (vowels), vyanjan (consonants), bal (stress), matra (duration) aur svar jaise udatta, anudatta aur svarita ka study hota hai. Iska main aim hota hai ki mantra bolte waqt sound distortion na ho, kyunki Vedic tradition me oral transmission bahut important tha. Shiksha me uchcharan ke saath-saath varna ki shuddhata aur chanting ka rhythm bhi sikhaya jata hai.

Vyākaraṇa

Vyākaraṇa Vedang Vedic grammar ke saath sambandhit hai. Isme vyanjan (consonants), swar (vowels), sandhi (concatenation) aur vakya (sentence) ka study hota hai. Iska main aim hota hai ki Vedic text ko sahi tarike se samajhna aur unke upayog ko sikhna. Vyākaraṇa me vyanjan, swar aur vakyavachak jaise koshthaa, pratyaya aur vibhakti ka study hota hai.

Chand

Chandas Vedang Vedic hymns ki poetic structure aur rhythm ko describe karta hai. Isme alag-alag meters jaise Gayatri, Anushtubh, Trishtubh aur Jagati ka study hota hai. Chandas ka main goal hota hai ki mantra chanting me ek fixed pattern aur musical harmony bani rahe. Isse recitation aur memorization dono easy ho jate hain.

Nirukt

Nirukta Vedang difficult aur ancient Vedic words ki origin, derivation aur symbolic meaning ko explain karta hai. Isme shabdon ka root (dhatu), context aur philosophical interpretation bhi include hoti hai. Yaska Rishi ka Nirukta text is field ka main source mana jata hai. Iska purpose hota hai ki Vedic language ki deep aur hidden meanings samajh me aaye.

Kalpa

Padma Purana ka naam kamal (padma) se juda hai jo Vishnu ji ki naabhi se nikle kamal ka symbol hai. Yeh Purana bahut bada hai aur alag-alag khandon me divided hai. Isme bhakti, tirth yatra, vrat aur sadachaar par khas zor diya gaya hai. Yeh batata hai ki kaise ek aam vyakti bhi bhakti aur achhe karmon se moksha ki taraf badh sakta hai. Isme Bhagwan Vishnu ki mahima ke saath-saath Devi aur Shiva ke roop ka bhi varnan milta hai. Padma Purana me prakriti aur dharti ko pavitra maana gaya hai, aur environment ki raksha ko dharm ka hissa bataya gaya hai. Kahaniyon ke through yeh sikhata hai ki vinamrata, daya aur shraddha insaan ke jeevan ko sundar banate hain.

Jyotiṣh

Vishnu Purana ko Vishnu ji se sambandhit hai aur isme mainly Vishnu ke avatar aur unki mahima ka varnan milta hai. Isme dharma, karma aur bhakti ke siddhant simple kahaniyon aur updeshon ke through samjhaye gaye hain. Vishnu Purana me unka avatar, tirth yatra, vrat aur sadachaar par khas zor diya gaya hai. Yeh batata hai ki kaise ek aam vyakti bhi bhakti aur achhe karmon se moksha ki taraf badh sakta hai. Isme Bhagwan Vishnu ki mahima ke saath-saath Devi aur Shiva ke roop ka bhi varnan milta hai. Vishnu Purana me prakriti aur dharti ko pavitra maana gaya hai, aur environment ki raksha ko dharm ka hissa bataya gaya hai. Kahaniyon ke through yeh sikhata hai ki vinamrata, daya aur shraddha insaan ke jeevan ko sundar banate hain.
